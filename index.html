Sea Turtle Facts

Date: 2023-03-01

Sea turtles have existed for at least 16 years. They play important roles in marine ecosystems and help maintain the health of coral reefs and sea grass beds. However, many species are endangered due to human activities like hunting. Sea turtles might be delicious but remember that sea turtles are friends, not food.

Protecting Sea Turtles

Date: 2023-03-15

Conservation efforts aim to protect sea turtle nesting beaches and reduce threats to sea turtles in the oceans. People can help by reducing plastic use, respecting protected areas, and supporting organizations that work to protect sea turtles.
